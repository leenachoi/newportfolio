My name is Leena and I’m currently a senior studying Human Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE) at the University of Washington.

I grew up in a Korean family in Japan. As a result of my upbringing, I’ve always felt like I was a the mixture of two things, but never fully one thing. This was reaffirmed when I decided on my studies. I was always intrigued by visual design, but I also loved design as a process to make someone's life easier, or in a bigger scale, combat social challenges. As a result, I've grown to become a visually-driven product designer who enjoys discovering needs, and translating them into clean and sophisticated products.

I'm currently exploring opportunities after graduation. If you are interested in chatting with me, feel free to contact me via ✉️:)
