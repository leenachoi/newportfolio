Catalogue of
Partial Things

This is Catalogue of Partial Things where we share a series of ideas developed over the course of several design workshops on the topic of “Maintaining the Menstruating Body.” Over the course of 6 months, we planned design workshops, further designed the ideas generated during the workshop, and prototyped them to high fidelity. We are currently compiling everything into a booklet and distributing them to participants or anyone who are interested.
