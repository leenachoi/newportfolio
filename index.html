About

Hello and welcome! I am Leena, a product designer, currently designing features and experiences for Microsoft Azure. I and my cat, Ash, are based in Seattle, WA.

I was born and raised in Japan, and moved to the US at the age of 15. I majored in Human-Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington. After graduating, I started my career designing at a startup in Seattle. After working there for a year, I took an opportunity to design at Microsoft. Currently, I am in Azure team, focusing on making complex features simple.

広島県出身。15歳でアメリカに移り住み、大学ではシアトルにあるワシントン大学のHuman-Centered Design & Engineering (HCDE)という学部でUXデザイン、ユーザーリサーチ、プログラミングを学ぶ。卒業後は、シアトルにあるスタートアップで１年間ほど働き、その後マイクロソフトへ転職。現在はAzureチームのUXデザイナーとして働いている。
