Chitchat is a mobile application designed to help users feel more comfortable expressing their thoughts in the workplace. This project started as my internship project at LINE. During the internship, I worked with two other interns, and we designed, developed, and launched a product to help people in some ways.

Due to my profound interest in this topic, I continued to work on this project individually after the internship.
